Effects of Global Warming and What is the Cause?

In the model above it shows the cause and effects of global warming and how common pollution like big companies are contributing to global warming.

To represent what climate change and pollution is doing to our earth we made a stop motion animation to show what that is happpening in a different way. We used our characters to represent the Earth and pollution. We made it so a fight broke out between the two because it can represent how us poluting us also uf fighting the world.

Storyboard

This is an image of our story board that we created to help us visualize our idea. The idea of our story was to have a fight between the main characters Oil guy and Earth guy. In the first square, you can see that the are starting to argue wth eachother which signifies how in the begining pollution was a problem but not that bad. As the story goes on, the intensity of the fight changes which shows how the pollution and climate change is also inceasing. This ultimately leads to Earth guys "death".

Our Code for the Lighting

	  forever(function () {
    light.setAll(0x0000ff)
    for (let index = 0; index <= 255; index++) {
        light.setBrightness(index)
        pause(20)
    }
    for (let index = 0; index <= 255; index++) {
        light.setBrightness(255 - index)
        pause(20)
    }
})

Shown above is the code we used for the lighting in our animation. This code was specifically used in the scene where the paper was showing the 5 year later time skip. We used it to brighten up the picture because it looked a little dull without it.
